Smarr Garage Door offers a wide variety of garage doors, operators, and garage door accessories, from Clopay, Wayne-Dalton, and LiftMaster

We understand that each house is different, and it is our goal to create custom solutions for each and every installation or repair. If you need a new garage door, there are many things to consider when deciding on the brand and model, such as color, window styles, insulation (R-value), and decorative hardware. Our friendly staff is available to help answer any questions you may have. Or you may research directly on our vendors' websites - many of them offer tools to help make your new garage door selection easy! Both Wayne-Dalton and Clopay offer tools that allow you to upload a picture of your home, and change the style of garage door to help you determine which one would be right for you.

  • Wayne-Dalton's Garage Door Design Center
  • Clopay's Door Imagination System

We also understand that not everyone needs or wants a new garage door. We try to repair garage doors whenever possible, making sure the end product is safe, and in a condition that will continue to serve your needs.
