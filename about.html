About Us

Our Company

Smarr Garage Door, Inc. was established in 1979 by Dale Smarr. Over the last 35 years, Dale has grown this business from a one man operation to the multi-truck company we are today, servicing most of central Missouri. In late 2014, the Company successfully transitioned to new ownership, Cade and Katie Smith. We plan to build on the foundation that Dale laid, and continue the growth of this Company by implementing Customer-centric policies, and creating processes to achieve outstanding results on every job.

Mission – What we do

Our mission is to provide high quality products and service to our residential and commercial Customers in central Missouri to drive Customer Satisfaction. We will do this through operational excellence, the Baldrige Criteria for Performance Excellence, and strong branding position.

Vision – Where we are going

To be the primary resource for service and installation of garage, and entry doors, in central Missouri.

Values – Non-negotiable

  • Honesty
  • Integrity
  • Humility
  • Patience
  • Loyalty
  • Positive Attitude

About the Owners

Cade Smith started in the garage door industry in 2005, with the last 4 years spent servicing the mid-Missouri area, mainly Columbia, MO. He is passionate about Customer Satisfaction, and will go out of his way to make sure a job is done right. As a U.S. Army veteran, Cade understands the meaning of hard work and sacrifice.

Cade's wife, Katie Smith, has an engineering background, and provides support in the Human Resources, Accounting, and IT functions of the Company.

Customer Satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive for the perfect door every time. If we have not met your expectations in any way, we will make it right. We are members of the Internation Door Association (IDA), and believe wholeheartedly in the Code of Conduct and business practices set forth by this association.
